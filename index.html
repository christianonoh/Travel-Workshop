The Afro Safari Expedition 2023

Embark on a journey of a lifetime with The Afro Safari Expedition 2023!
Explore the wonders of Afro culture through travel, fashion, food, workshops, and unforgettable safari experiences.
Join us for an adventure you'll never forget.

2023.12.10(SUN) ~ 20(WED)
@ Lagos, Cape Town and Kigali.

Main Program

Workshops

Participate in interactive workshops focused on Afro dance, music, art, fashion, and cuisine, led by experienced cultural experts.

Guided Tours

Explore the diverse landscapes and wildlife of Africa with guided tours to national parks, reserves, and historic sites. See the "big five" game animals

Network

Connect with fellow adventurers, and industry professionals from around the world, making valuable connections and new relationships.

Gift Bags

Attendees will receive gift bags filled with a variety of handcrafted souvenirs, including Ankara accessories, postcards, jewelry and more.

Closing Gala

The event will culminate with a gala dinner and celebration, featuring live music, dance performances, and a showcase of Afro-inspired fashion and art.
